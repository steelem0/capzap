About CatZap 🐾 What is CatZap? CatZap is a mood-based meme generator for cat lovers, emoji addicts, and anyone who needs a good meow-ment.

Pick your vibe → Get a cat → Zap it to your favorites or share with a friend 😻. 🔌 Powered by CATAAS We use the CATAAS API (Cat as a Service) to fetch our feline friends.
It's free, fun, and run by awesome people who love cats almost as much as you do. 🔐 Privacy Purr-spective We don’t collect, store, or ship out your data. Your favorites are saved right on your device using localStorage.
No accounts, no tracking, just pure cat joy. 👩‍💻 Built by Marcy Steele Creative technologist, digital product designer, and person responsible for all the animated hearts.
